Tribute to A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India & Former President

About Him

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam (1931–2015) was an Indian aerospace scientist who served as the 11th President of India. He played a key role in India’s missile and nuclear programs and was lovingly called the "Missile Man of India".

Achievements

  • Developed India's first Satellite Launch Vehicle (SLV-III).
  • Served as the 11th President of India (2002–2007).
  • Awarded Bharat Ratna, India’s highest civilian honor.
  • Inspired millions through his books like Wings of Fire.

Inspiration

Dr. Kalam’s life continues to inspire students, scientists, and leaders around the world. His simplicity, dedication, and vision for India’s future made him one of the most loved personalities in the country.
